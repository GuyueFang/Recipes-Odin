Croque Madame

Description

Croque madame is a French dish that is similar to a croque monsieur, but with the addition of a fried egg on top. It is made with ham, cheese, and bread, and is typically served as a quick and easy meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of ham
  • 2 slices of Gruy√®re cheese
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Place 1 slice of ham and 1 slice of cheese between 2 slices of bread to make a sandwich.
  3. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in flour until smooth, about 1 minute.
  4. Slowly whisk in milk, and cook, whisking constantly, until thickened, about 5 minutes.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  6. Place sandwich on a baking sheet, and spoon sauce over the top.
  7. Bake in preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 5 minutes.
  8. Meanwhile, fry an egg in a skillet over medium heat until yolk is set but still runny.
  9. Place fried egg on top of sandwich, and serve immediately.

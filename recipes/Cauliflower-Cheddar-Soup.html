Cauliflower Cheddar Soup

Description

Cauliflower cheddar soup is a creamy, cheesy soup that is perfect for a cold day. It is made with cauliflower, cheddar cheese, and a few other ingredients to create a rich and flavorful soup that is sure to warm you up.

Ingredients

  • 1 head cauliflower, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. In a large pot, combine cauliflower, onion, garlic, and chicken broth.
  2. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until cauliflower is tender.
  3. Using an immersion blender, blend the soup until smooth.
  4. Stir in heavy cream, cheddar cheese, salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper.
  5. Simmer until cheese is melted and soup is heated through.
  6. Season with additional salt and pepper to taste.
